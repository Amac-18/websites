More About the GTR-35

The Nissan GT-R R35, introduced as a high-performance sports car, is famous for its impressive speed, advanced technology, and sleek design. Under the hood, it features a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V6 engine that delivers 565 horsepower in the standard model, while the GT-R Nismo variant boosts this to 600 horsepower.The torque stands at 467 lb-ft for the standard version and 481 lb-ft for the Nismo. Both versions are equipped with a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission with paddle shifters and an all-wheel-drive system, allowing the car to achieve a 0-60 mph time of approximately 2.9 seconds for the standard model and an even faster 2.5 seconds for the Nismo. The top speed is around 196 mph.

In terms of size, the GT-R R35 measures 4,710 mm in length, 1,895 mm in width, and 1,370 mm in height, with a wheelbase of 2,780 mm. Its curb weight is around 3,933 lbs, with the Nismo model being slightly lighter. The chassis is fitted with fully independent suspension, featuring adjustable Bilstein dampers, and the braking system is enhanced with Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes on the Nismo version, ensuring superior handling and control.
